# Rules

Submissions must be made before the end of Phase 1. You may submit **5
submissions per day** and **100 in total** during the development phase. In the
final phase your last development-phase submission is forwarded automatically;
no new submission is accepted.

This challenge is governed by the
[general ChaLearn contest rules](http://www.causality.inf.ethz.ch/GeneralChalearnContestRuleTerms.html).

The dataset is derived from the public-domain NHANES survey (U.S. CDC / NCHS);
see the **Data** page for licence details.
