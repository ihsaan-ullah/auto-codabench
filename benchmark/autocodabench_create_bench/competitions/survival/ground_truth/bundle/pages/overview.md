# Solve a Survival Analysis Problem

_Brought to you by the Survival Team._

In this challenge we tackle a survival-analysis problem from a machine-learning
perspective. The goal of survival analysis is to predict the expected time
before a given event occurs. Here we estimate the survival time of patients
given data from the NHANES dataset.

## What you submit

This is a sample, **code-submission** competition. During the competition you
may submit any of:

- only prediction results (no code),
- a pre-trained prediction model, or
- a prediction model that is trained and tested in the sandbox.

Submissions are scored automatically with the concordance index (see
**Evaluation**). Download the starting kit and a small debug dataset from the
**Files** tab to make your first submission.
